# Doe引入

DSOs can employ alternative indirect approaches to maintain the integrity of the networks with high DER penetration. 

> DSO 可以采用替代的间接方法来维持具有高 DER 渗透率的网络的integrity。

One of these solutions, which is being trialed by network operators in Australia (e.g., [20], [21]) is the concept of operating envelopes that limit the imports/exports of prosumers to ensure network integrity. 

> 澳大利亚的网络运营商正在试验的解决方案之一（例如，[20]、[21]）是操作范围的概念，它限制产消者的进口/出口，以确保网络的integrity。

In [20], the operating envelope is defined as “a principled allocation of the available hosting capacity to the individuals or aggregate DER or connection points within a segment of a distribution network in each time interval”’.

> 在[20]中，OE被定义为“在每个时间间隔内将可用托管容量原则性地分配给分配网络的一段内的个人或聚合分布式能源或连接点”。

Instead, prosumers manage their assets individually within their assigned DOEs to trade in the LEM using their own management systems.

> 相反，产消者在其指定的 DOE 内单独管理其资产，以便使用自己的管理系统在 LEM 中进行交易。

# OE优势：DSO不直接参与价格结算

Prosumers can participate in the LEM to trade energy with their peers in a decentralized fashion within the DOEs without the explicit engagement of the DSO in price settlement.

> 产消者可以参与 LEM，在 DOE 内以去中心化的方式与同行进行能源交易，而无需 DSO 明确参与价格结算。

# DSO直接取消交易的劣势

Although it is an effective mechanism to maintain network integrity, prosumers may get demotivated if they get blocked repeatedly due to their physical location in the connected network. Moreover, EV management is not considered which can also be used for P2P contracts.

> 尽管这是维护网络完整性的有效机制，但如果产消者由于其在连接网络中的物理位置而反复被阻止，他们可能会失去动力。
