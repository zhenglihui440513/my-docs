# DER两面性

DERs provide both a challenge and an opportunity for the electricity industry. Unchecked widespread adoption of DERs could quickly over-burden infrastructure and challenge existing distribution utility practices. Time sensitive peaks, reversed power flows, and resource visibility are just a few of the major issues that need to be addressed for costeffective DER integration [3]. On the upside, DERs present an opportunity to unlock flexibility and value stacking from price responsive prosumers when efficient incentive mechanisms are available [4]. Significant questions remain regarding how prosumers may participate in the overall energy system and markets

> 分布式能源为电力行业既带来了挑战，也带来了机遇。如果不受限制地广泛采用分布式能源，可能很快就会使基础设施负担过重，并对现有的配电设施做法提出挑战。时间敏感的峰值、反向潮流和资源可视性只是具有成本效益的 DER 集成需要解决的几个主要问题 [3]。从好的方面来说，当有效的激励机制可用时，分布式能源提供了一个机会，可以从价格敏感的产消者那里释放灵活性和价值叠加[4]。关于产消者如何参与整个能源系统和市场仍然存在重大问题
